Our purpose

We exist to find the truth.

In a world where a single headline can move markets, reputations and lives, the truth has never been harder to see — or more valuable to hold. Press Detective was built on one conviction: that the truth is knowable, and that it is worth the work.

We don't deal in opinions, spin or assumptions. Every claim is tested against evidence, every source is traced to its origin, and every finding is checked by legal and financial experts before it reaches you. Whether it's a press article, a counterparty, a transaction or a dispute, we look past the surface and follow the facts wherever they lead — so you can act on what's real, not on what's reported.
