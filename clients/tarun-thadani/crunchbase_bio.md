## Full bio (Crunchbase "Overview" / long description field)

Tarun Thadani is an entrepreneur and the Founder and Group CEO of Dharte (dharte.com), an
integrated wellness and sustainability marketplace headquartered in Mumbai, India.

Dharte connects consumers with yoga practitioners, natural medicine healers, sustainable brands,
nutrition experts, and spiritual teachers across a unified digital and physical platform. Physical
wellness spaces operated under Dharte include a Wellness Centre in Worli, a Yoga Shala on Madh
Island (Mumbai), and Dharte House retreat in Dharamkot, Dharamshala.

Thadani built Dharte by transforming Silkworks, the Mumbai home furnishings business founded by
his late father Prakash A. Thadani in 1989. Following the family business's three-decade run in
the home décor market, Thadani pivoted the organisation toward wellness in 2021, recognising the
fragmented and underserved nature of India's holistic health sector.

Prior to Dharte, Thadani founded and operated KPT Hospitality, whose flagship brand Cool Chef
established itself in Mumbai's food and beverages sector.

Thadani founded and organises Dharte Fest, an annual Sustainability and Wellness Expo held in
Mumbai. The 2025 edition took place at The Bombay Presidency Radio Club, Colaba, in September 2025.
