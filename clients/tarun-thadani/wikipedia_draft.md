## Dharte

**Dharte** is an Indian wellness and sustainability marketplace founded and headquartered in
Mumbai, Maharashtra. It connects consumers with practitioners, brands, and venues across
yoga, natural medicine, sustainable living, nutrition, spiritual practices, and holistic health.

### History

Dharte traces its roots to **Silkworks**, a home furnishings retail business established in
Mumbai in 1989 by Prakash A. Thadani. Silkworks became a well-known fixture in Mumbai's
home décor market over three decades.

Tarun Thadani, the eldest son of the founder, assumed leadership of the business following
his father's death. In 2021, Thadani rebranded the company as **Dharte**, pivoting from
home furnishings to a digital and physical wellness marketplace. The Silkworks furnishings
brand has continued to operate as a subsidiary.

### Founder

**Tarun Thadani** is an entrepreneur based in Mumbai, Maharashtra, India. He is the Group
CEO and founder of Dharte. Prior to the wellness marketplace, he operated **KPT Hospitality**,
which includes **Cool Chef**, a hospitality brand in Mumbai's food and beverages sector.

Thadani has described Dharte's mission as bridging the fragmented Indian wellness landscape
by connecting seekers, facilitators, brands, and physical venues on a single platform.

### Operations

Dharte operates physical wellness spaces in Mumbai, including a Wellness Centre in Worli
and a Yoga Shala on Madh Island. The company also operates **Dharte House**, a retreat
property in Dharamkot, Dharamshala, Himachal Pradesh.

### Events

**Dharte Fest** is an annual one-day Sustainability and Wellness Expo organised by Dharte.
The 2025 edition was held on 27 September 2025 at The Bombay Presidency Radio Club,
Colaba, Mumbai.
