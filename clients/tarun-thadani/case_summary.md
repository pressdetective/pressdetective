2. **No role** — only sent invitations (innocuous; no offence).
3. **Altered, delayed complaint** — extortion absent originally, added ~2 months later.
4. **Procedural illegality** — no accused examined, no CDR/messages/bank verification before FIR.
5. **Complainant antecedents** — pattern of alleged misuse of process (subject to relevance/admissibility).
