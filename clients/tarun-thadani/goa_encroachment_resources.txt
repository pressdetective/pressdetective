## Complaint Checklist — Include in Every Email

1. Survey number / Sy. No. of the property
2. Village and Taluka name
3. GPS coordinates or Google Maps link
4. Photographs with date/time stamps
5. Description of violation (tree cutting, wall construction, landfill, hill cutting)
6. Names of encroachers if known
7. Reference to applicable law:
   - Goa Land Revenue Code 1968
   - Goa Preservation of Trees Act 1984
   - Town & Country Planning Act 1974

## Media Approach Strategy

When emailing these desks, include:
1. **Strong subject line:** e.g. *"Illegal Tree Felling + Property Encroachment at [Location] — Documents & Photos Available"*
2. Attach photographs with GPS metadata/timestamps
3. State the survey number and authorities notified (but haven't acted)
4. Mention NGO support (e.g. "Goa Foundation has been informed")
5. Offer to speak on record or provide an exclusive

**The three-way pressure combination that works:**
Complaint Filed + Media Coverage + NGO Backing = Government action

# Sample Complaint Letter — Illegal Property Encroachment & Tree Cutting

---

**To:**
[Authority Name]
[Designation]
[Address]

**CC:**
[Other authorities / NGOs / Media]

**Subject:** Complaint regarding Illegal Property Encroachment and Unauthorised Tree Cutting at Survey No. [XXX], Village [XXX], Taluka [XXX], Goa

**Date:** ___________

---

Dear Sir/Madam,

I, [Your Full Name], residing at [Your Address], hereby bring to your urgent attention the ongoing illegal encroachment of property and unauthorised felling of trees at the following location:

**Location Details:**
- Survey Number: [Sy. No. XXX]
- Village: [Village Name]
- Taluka: [Taluka Name]
- District: [North / South Goa]
- GPS Coordinates: [Latitude, Longitude]
- Google Maps Link: [Link]

**Nature of Violation:**
[Describe clearly — e.g.:
- Illegal construction of compound wall on comunidade/government/private land without panchayat permission
- Unauthorised felling of [X] trees without Tree Authority permission under the Goa Preservation of Trees Act, 1984
- Illegal landfilling / hill cutting at the said survey number
- Blocking of public footpath / road]

**Timeline:**
The encroachment/tree cutting was observed on [Date(s)]. Despite [any prior complaints filed], no action has been taken to date.

**Legal Provisions Violated:**
1. Goa, Daman and Diu Preservation of Trees Act, 1984
2. Goa Land Revenue Code, 1968
3. Town and Country Planning Act, 1974
4. [If applicable] Coastal Regulation Zone (CRZ) Notification, 2019

**Relief Sought:**
1. Immediate stop-work notice to the encroacher
2. Physical inspection of the site by the concerned officer
3. Registration of FIR against the violators under applicable law
4. Restoration of the land/trees to original condition
5. Written acknowledgment of this complaint within [7] days

I enclose the following supporting documents:
- Photographs (Annexure A) — dated [Date]
- Survey map / property documents (Annexure B)
- [Any previous complaint copies] (Annexure C)

I request urgent action in this matter and reserve the right to approach the National Green Tribunal / Bombay High Court (Goa Bench) / Goa Lokayukta in the event of inaction.

Yours sincerely,

[Full Name]
[Address]
[Phone]
[Email]
[Date]

---

*Note: Send by email with read receipt + physical registered post to key authorities.*
