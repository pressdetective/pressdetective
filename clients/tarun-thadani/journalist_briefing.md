## THE STORY IN ONE PARAGRAPH

A Mumbai wellness startup founder has been entangled in a fraudulent FIR filed by
a businessman he lent money to. The complainant — Abhishek Badriprasad Saraf —
has documented prior fraud cases against him, including one before the
Anti-Corruption Bureau and disputes with a Martin Burn Group entity. Despite Tarun
Thadani being away from the alleged incident location at the time, Saraf named him
in FIR 0654/2022 (Dadar PS), leading to a chargesheeting. The co-accused's Section
528 BNSS quashing petition is filed. Thadani's petition is in preparation.
