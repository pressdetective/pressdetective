## ABOUT SECTION (paste this into your LinkedIn "About")

Tarun Thadani is the founder and CEO of Dharte (dharte.com), India's curated
wellness marketplace connecting practitioners, studios, and seekers across yoga,
Ayurveda, meditation, and holistic health.

Tarun comes from a textile family — his parents founded Silkworks in 1989 and built
it into a respected fabric house serving India's fashion and home-décor industry.
Drawing on three decades of watching his family build supplier and customer
relationships with integrity, Tarun brought the same philosophy to the digital
wellness economy.

Dharte launched in 2021 as the first vertically-integrated Indian wellness platform
to combine verified practitioner listings, bookable sessions, and a practitioner
community in one place. Today it serves members across Mumbai, Pune, Goa, Bangalore,
and the UK diaspora.

Tarun believes in long-term platform thinking: real members, honest listings, and
on-platform journeys that respect both practitioners and seekers. He is based in
Mumbai.

**Open to:** Wellness brand partnerships | Investor conversations | Collaborations
with practitioners and studios | Media briefings on India's wellness economy
