## 4. Legal / Compliance Notes

- All letters are written under Press Council of India guidelines on accuracy, fairness, and completeness of reporting.
- The constitutional basis cited (implicitly) is the right to informational privacy under *K.S. Puttaswamy v. Union of India* (2017) — not stated in the letters themselves but available for escalation if needed.
- The 14-day response window is standard for press correction correspondence. If no response is received by 26 June 2026, the next step is a formal complaint to the Press Council of India (PCI) accompanied by these letters as exhibits.
- Sub-judice caution: no letter makes any assertion about guilt or innocence in any pending matter; all statements are confined to verified procedural facts (clean chit, HC bail).
- GDPR / DPDP note: these letters contain personal data about Mr Vora. They should be sent by Press Detective to the named editors only and not circulated further without Gautam's consent.
