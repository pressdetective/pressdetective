## Suggested sequence

1. **Re-file** the *Updated Master Complaint* with the *Evidence Packet* attached (so the strengthened version and the precedent are on record).
2. If no acknowledgement in ~10–15 days, send the *Reminder*.
3. If still no action, send the *Formal Appeal* to the Chairman **and** the North Goa Collector together.
4. In parallel, circulate the *Open Letter* to press and supporters and push the petition.

## A note on the recipient lists

The institutional list is fine to email (BCC). For the named-supporter list, reach out **individually and respectfully** rather than mass-blasting personal inboxes — a short personal note asking them to sign/share the petition lands far better and avoids spam complaints. Always BCC, include who you are, and honour any request to be removed.

## Key facts used (verify before filing)

- Noise: **68–75 dB(A)** measured during play vs. **55 dB(A)** daytime / **45 dB(A)** night residential limits — Noise Pollution (Regulation and Control) Rules, 2000.
- Operating roughly **7 a.m. to midnight**; impact noise + amplified music + rowdy conduct.
- Legal hooks: 2000 Rules; Environment (Protection) Act, 1986; **Section 133 CrPC / Section 152 BNSS, 2023**; **Article 21** (right to peaceful living/sleep — *In re: Noise Pollution* (2005) 5 SCC 733).
- Precedent on the same plot: **Village Panchayat Siolim-Sodiem Revocation Order dated 24.09.2008** (Sy. No. 197/7), following **Mr. Olympio Almeida's** complaint and a TCP Mapusa finding of construction "not as per approved plan."
