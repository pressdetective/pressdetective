About

Prateek Gupta has spent over two decades building and operating commodity trading businesses across Asia, the Middle East, Europe, and the United Kingdom. His career has centred on base metals — particularly nickel — and the trade finance structures that underpin large-scale physical commodity transactions.

After joining the family business, Ushdev International, he became Managing Director following the passing of his father Vijay Gupta in 2009. He subsequently built TMT Metals into an internationally active trading platform with operations in Switzerland, Singapore, Dubai, and London.

His professional focus has been the origination and structuring of physical commodity deals across LME-grade metals, with deep experience in counterparty relationships, trade finance facilities, and cross-border commercial operations.

Areas of Expertise

Base Metals Trading

Physical nickel, copper, and ferrous metals. LME-grade contracts, spot and forward structures, containerised cargo logistics.

Trade Finance

Structured commodity finance, revolving credit facilities, letters of credit, and multi-bank syndicated arrangements.

Cross-Border Transactions

Transactions spanning India, Singapore, Switzerland, Dubai, and the UK. Multi-jurisdiction compliance and counterparty management.

Market Analysis

Nickel supply/demand dynamics, LME pricing, Asian production flows, and the impact of EV battery demand on base metal markets.
