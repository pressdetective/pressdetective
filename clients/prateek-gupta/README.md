## Ground rules carried over (please keep)
- The legal track (renewed appeal to the Court of Appeal via solicitors) is the only route that can
  challenge the finding. Confirm representation and the appeal/extension-of-time deadline first.
- Do NOT assert innocence as established fact, and do NOT re-accuse the court-cleared individuals —
  a court found them innocent; doing so is defamatory and would harm any appeal.
- Genuine new evidence goes to counsel and the court, never to the press first.
- No mass outreach to publications, officials, or journalists.
- Public statements should be cleared by current solicitors.
