## About (full text — 1,200 chars, fits LinkedIn limit)

I have spent over twenty years building and running commodity trading businesses across
Asia, the Middle East, Europe, and the UK. My career has centred on base metals —
particularly nickel — and the trade finance structures that underpin large-scale physical
commodity transactions.

I founded TMT Metals and built it into an internationally active trading platform with
operations in Switzerland, Singapore, Dubai, and London, structuring physical nickel and
base metal transactions against LME-grade benchmarks.

Before TMT, I was Managing Director of Ushdev International, the Mumbai-headquartered
commodities business founded by my father Vijay Gupta, where I led metals trading,
international operations, and trade finance from 2009.

My professional background spans:
• Physical commodity origination and deal structuring — nickel, copper, ferrous metals
• Trade finance: revolving credit facilities, LCs, multi-bank syndications
• Cross-border commercial operations across India, Singapore, UAE, Switzerland, UK
• LME markets, containerised cargo, pricing and risk management

I welcome connections from professionals in commodities, trade finance, banking, metals
markets, and related industries.

📍 Based in Dubai | Open to collaboration
